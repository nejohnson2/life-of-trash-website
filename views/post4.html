New York + Trash = ?

As many newcomers to New York City have noticed, this city is not shy about its trash. Each day enormous piles of trash are left roadside for processing. The piles are so common that people pass them in the street without a thought.


Life of Trash attempts to map the path of everyday trash by using cellular technology to relay location data periodically throughout the trash process cycle. This location data is streamed into a live map which allows the user to gather up-to-date information on the current location of their trash.
