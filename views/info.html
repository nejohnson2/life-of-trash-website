Life of Trash

With just three simple steps, you can begin to track your trash and better understand where its life ends.

1. Download App. Coming Soon...

By downloading and installing the Life of Trash app, users can successfully watch online as their trash moves through the country. The app is currently available on the Android App Store and a similar version will soon be available via the Apple App Store.

2. Throw phone away.

After successfully downloading the Life of Trash app, simply launch the app and place the phone in the trash or recycling prior to pickup. Be sure to charge the phone fully and place the phone in a plastic bag to protect against any liquids which might damage the phone.

3 Track online. Coming soon...

Once the phone has been placed in the trash, visit Life of Trash website to begin tracking where your trash is traveling.
