Trash Education

Any step towards improving our waste footprint will require education. Education rests in many forms and the Trash Lab intends on exploring each one of these.


  • Classroom

    Both at the university level and elementary level, educational courses are a crucial step towards improving our waste system.

  • City Tours

    Visiting real places gives people a firsthand understanding of waste management. NYC is filled with amazing places to visit.

  • Workshops

    Hosting trash related workshops will include hands-on activities giving people the skills and tools to pursue better waste understanding.

  • Partnerships

    The DSNY is always in need of expanding and promoting it's projects. The Trash Lab can help facility and implement these programs.
