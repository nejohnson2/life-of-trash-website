How it works

Give us money and we'll help you understand the Life of Trash


  • Funding

    Funding the Trash Lab will come from a variety of resources including research grants, educational grants and support from various partnerships.

  • Timeline and Initial Needs

    To initialize the Trash Lab, we need approximately one year to fully develop our initiatives, a facility to base our activities and an operating budget that would support a small staff and testing research equipment.

  • Measurables and Deliverables

    The success of Trash Lab will be seen by the number of participants that engage in research projects as well as the reports that are generated by its research.
